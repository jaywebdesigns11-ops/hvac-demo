COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked.

How much does a furnace replacement cost in Washington?

+

Most full installations in the Seattle area range between $4,500 and $9,000 depending on efficiency rating and home size. We provide firm, fixed quotes with no hidden fees.

Do you offer free estimates?

+

Yes, for all new installations and system replacements. For repair calls, we have a flat diagnostic fee that is waived if you proceed with the repair.

How fast can you get to my home?

+

We offer same-day service for emergencies. On average, we respond to service requests in under 2 hours during business hours.

Yes. Our technicians are factory-trained on all major brands including Trane, Lennox, Carrier, Rheem, and more.
